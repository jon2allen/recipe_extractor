### Title

red_braised_chicken_chunks.txt - 红烧鸡块

### Objectives
- Learn to make authentic red-braised chicken chunks.
- Master the technique of frying chicken for optimal color and texture.
- Achieve the perfect balance of sweet, savory, and aromatic flavors.


### Ingredients

- 20 花椒 (huājiāo) - peppercorns
- 2 大料 (dàliào) - star anise
- 1 section 桂皮 (guìpí) - cassia bark
- 2 干辣椒 (gān làjiāo) - dried chilies
- 20g 姜 (jiāng) - ginger, smashed
- 2 sections 葱 (cōng) - scallions
- 20g 蒜 (suàn) - garlic
- Bamboo shoots (冬笋/春笋 - dōngsǔn/chūnsǔn), cut into batons
- Chicken legs and/or drumsticks (about 2 jin 4 liang - approximately 1200-1300g), cut into walnut-sized pieces
- 25g 酱油 (jiàngyóu) - soy sauce
- A small amount 老抽 (lǎochōu) - dark soy sauce (for color)
- 15g 料酒 (liàojiǔ) - cooking wine
- 15g 生豆油 (shēng dòuyóu) - raw soybean oil
- 15g 猪大油 (zhū dàyóu) - lard
- 20g 猪大油 (zhū dàyóu) - lard
- 15-20g 酱油 (jiàngyóu) - soy sauce
- Water
- 15-20g 冰糖 (bīngtáng) - rock sugar
- Salt to taste
- 15-20g 白糖 (báitáng) - white sugar
- 35g 白糖 (báitáng) - white sugar (if using 2 jin 4 liang chicken)
- Peas (豌豆 - wāndòu)
- MSG (味精 - wèijīng)


### Instructions
1. Marinate chicken: Cut chicken into walnut-sized pieces. Add salt, 25g soy sauce, a little dark soy sauce, and 15g cooking wine. Mix well and marinate for 10 minutes.
2. Fry chicken: Heat 15g soybean oil until it smokes and turns light yellow. Fry chicken in batches until golden brown.  Set aside.
3. Prepare aromatics: Heat 15g soybean oil and 20g lard. Add peppercorns, star anise, and cassia bark. Fry until fragrant.
4. Stir-fry aromatics and chicken: Add ginger, garlic, and dried chilies. Stir-fry until golden brown. Add the fried chicken and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
5. Add ingredients: Add bamboo shoots, 2 liang (approximately 100g) cooking wine, 15-20g soy sauce, water, rock sugar, and a small amount of dark soy sauce. Add salt and 15-20g white sugar.
6. Simmer: Bring to a boil, then simmer for 5 minutes. Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more white sugar (35g for 2 jin 4 liang chicken).
7. Reduce sauce: Simmer uncovered for 3 minutes to reduce the sauce.
8. Add peas: Add peas and cover for 2 minutes.
9. Finish: Remove aromatics. Add MSG. Thicken sauce with a cornstarch slurry.
