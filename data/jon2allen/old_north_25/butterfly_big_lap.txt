### Title

butterfly_big_lap.txt - 蝴蝶大拉皮 (Húdié dà lāpí)


### Objectives
- Learn to make authentic  蝴蝶大拉皮 (Húdié dà lāpí).
- Master the techniques for preparing the various components.
- Understand the importance of presentation for this dish.

### Instructions

1. Prepare the mustard paste: Mix mustard, salt, sugar, and boiling water into a paste. Cover and let sit for 30 minutes.
2. Prepare the egg mixture: Beat 2 eggs.
3. Prepare the starch slurry: Settle the water starch, remove excess water, and mix with potato starch.
4. Prepare the pan: Wipe the wok clean with paper towels.  Lightly oil.
5. Stir-fry the meat: Stir-fry shredded meat in a small amount of oil until separated and cooked through.
6. Season the meat: Add garlic, chili powder, salt, and yellow wine to the meat. Stir-fry until slightly thickened.
7. Add aromatics: Add scallions and ginger to the meat.
8. Add spices and sauces: Add Sichuan peppercorn powder, yellow wine, soy sauce, light soy sauce, rock sugar, and dark soy sauce. Stir-fry until a reddish-brown color.
9. Prepare the vegetables: Cut cucumber into 10cm long strips. Cut radish into 3mm thick, 6cm long slices. Soak wood ear mushrooms for 2 hours and shred finely.
10. Arrange the ingredients: Place the shredded wood ear mushrooms on a plate, arranging them in a decorative pattern.  Add the meat, making sure it's visible.
11. Add toppings: Add chili paste, garlic paste, and sesame seeds on top of the meat.
12. Prepare the sauce: Mix rice vinegar, a small amount of aged black vinegar, soy sauce, MSG, sugar, and salt.
13. Add the sauce: Pour the sauce over the arranged ingredients.
14. Garnish: Add chili oil (optional) and coriander.
