### Title

gongbao_jitding.txt - 宫保鸡丁 (Gōngbǎo Jīdīng)

### Objectives
- Learn to make authentic Gongbao Jiding (Kung Pao Chicken).
- Master the technique of creating the signature "lychee" flavor profile.
- Achieve tender, flavorful chicken with a vibrant color.

### Ingredients

- Chicken:  鸡肉 (Jīròu) -  about 150g, cut into 1.5cm cubes (use chicken breast or thigh, remove skin and tendons)
- Onion: 葱 (Cōng) -  適量, diced
- Ginger: 姜 (Jiāng) - 5-8g, diced
- Garlic: 蒜 (Suàn) - 5-8g, minced
- Doubanjiang (fermented chili bean paste): 豆瓣酱 (Dòubànjiàng) - 5-8g
- Soy sauce: 酱油 (Jiàngyóu) - 20g
- Salt: 盐 (Yán) - a pinch
- Chicken bouillon: 鸡精 (Jījīng) - a pinch
- Cornstarch: 淀粉 (Diànfěn) - 14-15g, mixed with water to create a slurry
- Water: 清水 (Qīngshuǐ) - 20g
- Salt: 盐 (Yán) - a pinch
- MSG (optional): 味精 (Wèijīng) - a pinch
- Rice vinegar: 米醋 (Mǐcù) - 25g
- Black vinegar: 老陈醋 (Lǎo Chéncù) - 10g
- Sugar: 白糖 (Báitáng) - to taste (for a sweet and sour flavor)
- Sesame oil: 香油 (Xiāngyóu) - a few drops
- Dark soy sauce (optional, for color): 老抽 (Lǎochōu) - a few drops
- Cornstarch slurry (for thickening): 芡 (Qiàn) - 2g (approx.), mixed with water
- Sichuan peppercorns: 花椒 (Huājiāo) - 20 peppercorns
- Dried chilies: 干辣椒 (Gānlàjiāo) - several segments
- Peanuts: 花生米 (Huāshēngmǐ) - a handful


### Instructions
1. Dice the onion, ginger, and garlic.
2. Cut the chicken into 1.5cm cubes.  Remove any skin or tendons from chicken thighs.
3. Marinate the chicken with soy sauce, salt, chicken bouillon, and cornstarch slurry.
4. Prepare the sauce: Mix water, salt, MSG (optional), rice vinegar, black vinegar, sugar, and sesame oil.  Add dark soy sauce for deeper color if desired.  Thicken with a small amount of cornstarch slurry.
5. Heat oil in a wok or pan over medium-low heat.
6. Add the chicken and stir-fry until golden yellow (approx. 3 minutes).
7. Add Sichuan peppercorns and dried chilies to the pan to release their aroma.
8. Add the onion, ginger, and garlic, stir-fry briefly until fragrant.
9. Pour the prepared sauce into the pan and stir to coat the chicken evenly.
10. Add peanuts and stir until heated through.
11. Serve immediately.
