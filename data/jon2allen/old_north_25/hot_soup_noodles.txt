### Title

hot_soup_noodles.txt - 热汤面条

### Objectives
- Learn to make delicious hot soup noodles for the seventh day of the lunar new year.
- Master the technique of making smooth and elastic noodles.
- Understand the importance of flavor layering in the soup.

### Ingredients

- 6两 (liang) noodles (approximately 360g)
- 3两2 (liang) water (approximately 192g)
- Salt (amount unspecified)
- 1/2 egg white (optional)
- Soybean oil (amount unspecified)
- Potatoes (amount unspecified)
- Napa cabbage (amount unspecified)
- Scallions (amount unspecified)
- Ginger (amount unspecified)
- Yellow wine (amount unspecified)
- Soy sauce (amount unspecified)
- Water (amount unspecified)
- MSG (amount unspecified)
- Pepper (amount unspecified)
- Black pepper (amount unspecified)
- Cilantro (amount unspecified)
- Chives (amount unspecified)


### Instructions

1. Mix the noodles with water, salt, and optionally half an egg white.
2. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic. Let it rest for 20 minutes, then knead again.
3. Heat soybean oil in a pan. Add potatoes and sauté for about 1 minute.
4. Add Napa cabbage and sauté for about 2 minutes.
5. Add scallions and ginger to the pan.
6. Add yellow wine and soy sauce.
7. Add water and bring to a boil. Simmer for 15 minutes.
8. Roll out the dough and cut it into noodles using a "扒楞刀" (bala knife) technique.
9. Cook the noodles.
10. Season the soup with MSG, pepper, and black pepper.
11. Garnish with cilantro and chives.
12. Serve the hot soup noodles.
