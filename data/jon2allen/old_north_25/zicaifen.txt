### Title

zicaifen.txt - Zicaifen (渍菜粉)

### Objectives
- Learn to make a traditional Northeastern Chinese Zicaifen dish.
- Master the technique of preparing the ingredients (garlic, ginger, onion, pork).
- Achieve the perfect balance of flavors: sour, crisp, and savory.


### Ingredients

- Garlic (蒜):  Amount to make small diced pieces (黄豆粒儿大小的碎丁)
- Ginger (姜): 3-5g, finely minced (小米粒状)
- Green Onions (葱):  thinly sliced (小眉毛葱)
- Pork Belly (五花肉):  Amount not specified, preferably "上五花" (hard rib)
- Pickled Cabbage (酸菜): Amount not specified, washed 1-2 times.
- Sweet Potato Starch Noodles (粉条/红薯粉): Amount not specified, soaked for 3 hours.
- Sichuan Peppercorns (花椒): Amount not specified
- Yellow Wine (黄酒): Amount not specified
- Soy Sauce (酱油): Amount not specified (some for stir-frying, some for the final dish)
- Rock Sugar (冰糖): Small amount (少点点)
- Dark Soy Sauce (老抽): Small amount (少点点)
- Bone Broth (骨头汤): ½ jin (approximately 250g, optional; water can be substituted)
- MSG (味精): Amount not specified
- Cooking Oil (材料油): Amount not specified (generous amount recommended)


### Instructions
1. Remove the stems from the garlic cloves and finely mince into small pieces, about the size of soybeans.
2. Mince the ginger into very small pieces.
3. Thinly slice the green onions.
4. Wash the pickled cabbage thoroughly (1-2 times).
5. Soak the sweet potato starch noodles in water for 3 hours.
6. Cut the pork belly into slices.
7. Heat cooking oil in a wok or pan and stir-fry the pork belly until some of the fat renders.
8. Add the minced garlic and ginger to the pan and stir-fry until fragrant.  Add a small amount of Sichuan peppercorn.
9. Add yellow wine and soy sauce to the pan.
10. Add the pickled cabbage to the pan.
11. Add ½ jin of bone broth (or water) to the pan.
12. Add the soaked sweet potato starch noodles to the pan.
13. Add a small amount of soy sauce, rock sugar, and dark soy sauce to adjust the color and flavor. Do not over-season.
14. Bring the mixture to a boil.  Adjust seasoning with salt and MSG.
15.  The finished dish should be sour, crisp, smooth, and have a savory flavor.
