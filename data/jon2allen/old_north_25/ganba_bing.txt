### Title

ganba_bing.txt - 干巴饼 (Gānbā bǐng)

### Objectives
- Learn to make delicious Ganba Bing (Dry Crisp Flatbread).
- Master the dough preparation technique for a soft and pliable texture.
- Create a flavorful filling using simple ingredients.
- Learn a unique rolling and serving method.

### Ingredients

- 8 liang (approximately 400g) all-purpose flour (面粉 - miànfěn)
- 1 liang (approximately 50g) boiling water (开水 - kāishuǐ)
- 3 liang (approximately 150g) cold water (凉水 - liángshuǐ)
- Vegetable oil (豆油 - dòuyóu)
- Scallions (葱 - cōng)
- Ginger (姜 - jiāng)
- Garlic (蒜 - suàn)
- Potatoes, julienned (土豆丝 - tǔdòusī)
- Salt (盐 - yán)
- Five-spice powder (五香粉 - wǔxiāngfěn) or Thirteen-spice powder (十三香 - shísānxīang)
- Red pepper (红椒 - hóngjiāo)
- Soy sauce (酱油 - jiàngyóu)
- Rice vinegar (陈醋 - chéncù)
- Fermented soybean paste (东北大豆酱 - dōngběi dàdòujiàng)
- Soy sauce (生抽酱油 - shēng chōu jiàngyóu)
- MSG (味精 - wèijīng) (optional)
- Pickled vegetables (咸菜 - xiáncài) (optional)
- Coriander (香菜 - xiāngcài) (optional)


### Instructions
1. Mix 8 liang of flour with 1 liang of boiling water and 3 liang of cold water to form a dough.
2. Knead the dough for about 2 minutes until smooth and elastic.
3. Let the dough rest for 10 minutes.
4. Knead the dough again briefly.
5. Divide the dough into equal-sized portions.
6. Roll each portion into a round shape, about 2mm thick.
7. Heat a flat pan or griddle over medium-high heat.
8. Cook the flatbread on the hot pan until bubbles appear and it turns golden brown, flipping once.  This should take about 2 minutes per side.
9. While the flatbread cooks, prepare the filling: Heat oil in a pan, sauté scallions, ginger, and garlic.
10. Add julienned potatoes, salt, five-spice or thirteen-spice powder, red pepper, and a little soy sauce. Stir-fry for about 3 minutes.
11. Add a dash of rice vinegar.
12. Prepare the sauce: Mix fermented soybean paste, soy sauce, and MSG (optional).
13. Spread the sauce on the cooked flatbread.
14. Add the potato filling, pickled vegetables (optional), coriander (optional), and scallions.
15. Roll the flatbread tightly.
16. Serve and enjoy!
