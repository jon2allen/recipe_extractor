### Title

demoli_braised_live_fish.txt - 得莫利炖活鱼

### Objectives
- Learn to prepare and braise a delicious live fish dish.
- Master the technique of preparing the fish for optimal flavor.
- Understand the importance of balancing flavors and colors in the dish.

### Ingredients

- Live fish (cleaned)
- 1 tbsp (25g) soy sauce (酱油) 酱油
- 1 tbsp (25g) dark soy sauce (老抽) 老抽
- 1 tbsp soybean paste (大豆酱) 大豆酱
- Tofu (嫩豆腐) 嫩豆腐
- Red and green peppers (红绿椒) 红绿椒 or green pepper (青椒) 青椒
- Perilla (紫苏) 紫苏
- Garlic (蒜) 蒜
- Ginger (姜) 姜
- Scallions (葱) 葱
- Cooking oil (油) 油
- Salt (盐) 盐
- MSG (味精) 味精
- Cooking wine (黄酒) 黄酒
- Sichuan peppercorns (花椒) 花椒
- Star anise (大料) 大料
- Pork belly (五花肉) 五花肉
- Napa cabbage stalk (白菜帮) 白菜帮
- Napa cabbage leaves (白菜叶) 白菜叶
- Starch noodles (粉条子) 粉条子


### Instructions

1. Cut the tofu into rectangular pieces.
2. Finely chop the perilla.
3. Clean the fish thoroughly, score the fish body with 1cm-deep cuts, and split the fish head.
4. Season the fish inside and out with salt and soy sauce, and marinate for 20 minutes.
5. After marinating, wipe off excess soy sauce from the fish.
6. Heat oil in a pan, add a pinch of salt to prevent sticking, and pan-fry the fish for 2 minutes per side.
7. Remove the fish from the pan and set aside.  Reserve the oil in the pan.
8. Add Sichuan peppercorns and star anise to the pan.
9. Add pork belly and stir-fry until golden brown.
10. Add red pepper (or green pepper), garlic, ginger, and scallions. Continue stir-frying.
11. Add cooking wine and soybean paste.
12. Add the pan-fried fish to the pan.
13. Add Napa cabbage stalk and leaves, and tofu.
14. Cover and simmer for 3 minutes.
15. After 3 minutes of simmering, add starch noodles and continue to simmer for another 15 minutes.
16. Check the sauce consistency.
17. Add MSG and drizzle the sauce over the ingredients.
18. Add red and green peppers.
19. Add perilla to one side of the dish.
20. Cover and simmer for another 3 minutes.
