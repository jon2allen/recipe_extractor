### Title

yu_tou_men_kuan_fen.txt - Fish Head Braised with Wide Noodles

### Objectives
- Learn to make delicious Fish Head Braised with Wide Noodles.
- Master the technique of creating a rich and flavorful broth.
- Understand the importance of proper cooking time for tender fish.

### Ingredients

- Fish head (amount unspecified)
- 20g white sugar
- 40g cooking oil (bean oil)
- Sichuan peppercorns (amount unspecified)
- Star anise (amount unspecified)
- Chili segments (amount unspecified)
- Scallions (amount unspecified)
- Ginger (amount unspecified)
- Garlic (amount unspecified)
- 1 Liang (approximately 50ml) Shaoxing rice wine
- 1 Liang (approximately 50ml) soy sauce
- Sugar color (amount unspecified)
- Rock sugar (amount unspecified)
- Dark soy sauce (amount unspecified)
- Salt (amount unspecified)
- White sugar (amount unspecified)
- Fermented soybean paste (amount unspecified)
- MSG (amount unspecified)
- Wide noodles (amount unspecified)
- Fresh coriander (amount unspecified)


### Instructions

1. Score the fish head with a "finger-width" cut.
2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the fish head for 2 minutes per side to remove fishy odor.
3. Caramelize 20g of white sugar until golden brown.
4. Heat 40g of bean oil in a pan.  Add Sichuan peppercorns, star anise, chili segments, scallions, ginger, and garlic. Stir-fry until fragrant.
5. Add the fried fish head to the pan.
6. Add 1 Liang of Shaoxing rice wine and 1 Liang of soy sauce.
7. Cover and simmer for another 2 minutes.
8. Add the caramelized sugar to the pan.
9. Add the broth (amount unspecified), bring to a boil for 1 minute.
10. Adjust the color by adding rock sugar and dark soy sauce.
11. Season with salt, white sugar, and fermented soybean paste.
12. When the fish is almost cooked, push it to the side to expose the fish head.
13. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
14. Thicken the sauce slightly.
15. Add wide noodles and cook until almost transparent.
16. Add MSG.
17. Garnish with fresh coriander.
