### Title

jiangdagu.txt - Braised Pork Bone

### Objectives
- Learn to make delicious and flavorful braised pork bone.
- Master the technique of creating a rich and glossy sauce.
- Understand the importance of selecting and preparing the ingredients.


### Ingredients

- 棒骨 (bàng gǔ) - Pork bone (amount not specified)
- 香叶 (xiāng yè) - Bay leaves (6-8 pieces)
- 大料 (dà liào) - Star anise (3-4 pieces)
- 栀子 (zhī zi) - Gardenia (2 pieces, optional)
- 花椒 (huā jiāo) - Sichuan peppercorns (30 pieces)
- 白蔻 (bái kòu) - White cardamom (15 pieces, optional)
- 山楂片 (shān zhā piàn) - Hawthorn slices (amount not specified)
- 山里红 (shān lǐ hóng) - Hawthorn (amount not specified)
- 草果 (cǎo guǒ) - Grains of Paradise (amount not specified)
- 红椒 (hóng jiāo) - Red pepper (amount not specified)
- 姜 (jiāng) - Ginger (30g)
- 葱 (cōng) - Scallions (1.5-2 taels, about 75-100g)
- 蒜 (suàn) - Garlic (amount not specified)
- 豆油 (dòu yóu) - Soybean oil (amount not specified, but at least 40g)
- 糖 (táng) - Sugar (40g)
- 黄酒 (huáng jiǔ) - Yellow wine (amount not specified)
- 酱油 (jiàng yóu) - Soy sauce:
    - 零添加酱油 (líng tiān jiā jiàng yóu) - No-additive soy sauce (25g)
    - 生抽 (shēng chōu) - Light soy sauce (20g)
    - 老抽 (lǎo chōu) - Dark soy sauce (amount not specified)
- 大豆酱 (dà dòu jiàng) - Soybean paste (30g)
- 盐 (yán) - Salt (amount not specified)
- 白糖 (bái táng) - White sugar (amount not specified)
- 红曲米 (hóng qū mǐ) - Red yeast rice (20g) or 红曲粉 (hóng qū fěn) - Red yeast powder (10-15g)
- 香油 (xiāng yóu) - Sesame oil (small amount)


### Instructions

1. Clean the pork bones thoroughly under running water.
2. Prepare the aromatics: wash and prepare bay leaves, star anise, gardenia (optional), Sichuan peppercorns, white cardamom (optional), hawthorn slices, hawthorn, grains of paradise, and red pepper.  Mince ginger and garlic. Chop scallions into segments.
3. Heat soybean oil in a pot. Add sugar and stir-fry until caramelized (40g sugar used in this recipe).
4. Add pork bones and stir-fry until lightly browned.
5. Add yellow wine, soy sauces (light, dark, and no-additive), soybean paste, salt, white sugar, and red yeast rice or powder. Stir well.
6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
7. Remove the aromatics and set aside.
8. Continue to simmer for another 10 minutes, covered.  Total simmering time is 20 minutes.
9. Remove about half of the braising liquid.  The remaining liquid should be slightly thickened.
10. Return the aromatics to the pot.
11. Arrange the braised pork bones on a serving plate.
12. Drizzle with sesame oil and serve.
